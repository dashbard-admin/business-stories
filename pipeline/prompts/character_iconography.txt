You are briefing a comic-book illustrator about the iconic visual
identity of {founder}, the founder/protagonist of {company}.

Based on what you know from public photographs and press coverage,
describe their iconic visual markers — the things a reader would
recognise them by at a glance.

Focus on:
- Hair: shape, length, colour, hairline if notable
- Facial hair: kind, density, style
- Signature glasses, hats, or other facial accessories
- Signature clothing: a specific garment they are known for
  (black turtleneck, hoodie, suit-no-tie, bohemian linen, etc.)
- Signature posture or stance: how they typically hold themselves
- Signature props or environment: a specific phone, a podium,
  a stage backdrop, a recurring object

Do NOT describe individual facial proportions, exact age in years,
or anything requiring photorealistic likeness. The goal is
COMIC-BOOK ICONOGRAPHY — the iconic markers, not the face. A
reader should be able to recognise this person from a silhouette
plus the markers, without needing a photoreal portrait.

If you genuinely do not have enough information about this person
to be specific — for example, a private founder with no public
photographs — return an empty iconography string. Do NOT fabricate
markers you are not confident about.

OUTPUT FORMAT — JSON object only, no prose, no code fences:
{{
  "iconography": "<60-100 word paragraph the illustrator can paste
                   directly into a prompt; starts with the most
                   distinctive marker; no second-person; no preamble;
                   do not use the person's name inside the paragraph.
                   Empty string if you cannot be specific.>"
}}
